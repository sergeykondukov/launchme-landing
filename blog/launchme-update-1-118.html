What updates 1.113 through 1.118 bring to LaunchMe users

A focused release cycle that adds a lot of new features at once. We introduced new widgets with deep customization, made Liquid Glass available to everyone, and added quality-of-life improvements that make daily use of LaunchMe on Mac faster and more flexible.

The main focus of these updates was widgets and visual personalization. Here is everything new in LaunchMe versions 1.113 through 1.118.

Music Widget: Minimal, Full Cover, and Vinyl

We added a new Music Widget with three distinct styles:

Full Cover
Shows the album cover of the track you are listening to. Control playback directly from the widget without switching apps.

Minimal
A clean layout for minimalists: song title and artist name only, with compact playback controls.

Vinyl
A retro-style layout that displays album art as a spinning vinyl record. Control music playback right from the widget.

Customization
Change widget size, background color, and text color. Choose which music player the widget connects to.

Album color sync
The Music Widget can automatically match its background to the dominant color of the current album cover, so the widget always feels in sync with what is playing.

Weather Widget: 8 styles, 4 sizes, unlimited customization

The new Weather Widget comes in 8 visual styles and 4 widget sizes. Pick a layout that fits your grid, then fine-tune colors, city, and background until it matches your Space.

Available styles:

  • Glass - transparent glass effect with a modern, lightweight look
  • Modern - clean lines and balanced typography that works on any desktop
  • Minimal - only the essentials: temperature and current conditions
  • Simple - straightforward layout with clear, easy-to-read weather info
  • Photo - set a custom photo background from built-in options or your own image
  • Classic - traditional weather widget layout with full forecast details
  • Slim - narrow and compact, ideal for tight grid layouts
  • Wide - spacious horizontal layout for larger widget slots

Each style supports its own customization options. Combine styles and sizes across different Spaces, or place multiple Weather Widgets on the same page for work, travel, and home cities.

Liquid Glass: Tinted, Clear, and custom colors

Liquid Glass is now available for Free accounts, not just Pro. Global Liquid Glass settings let you control the entire launcher look from one place:

Tinted
Pick from a range of tint colors for the glass effect and adjust opacity to match your wallpaper or brand style.

Clear
A transparent glass look that lets your wallpaper show through with minimal visual weight.

Custom colors
Go beyond presets and set your own tint for a launcher that feels uniquely yours.

Invoke with Gesture, now in Settings

Invoke with Gesture is fully integrated into LaunchMe Settings. Set up trackpad or Magic Mouse gestures to open and close the launcher alongside Hot Key and Hot Corners. No hidden setup steps: configure, test, and use gestures from the same settings panel as everything else.

Emoji and Symbols panel in the search bar

A new Emoji and Symbols panel lives inside the search bar. Browse and insert emojis or symbols while naming folders, Workflows, Spaces, or widget text, without leaving LaunchMe or opening another app.

Show over fullscreen apps

A new setting, Show over fullscreen apps, lets you open LaunchMe on any desktop even when another app is in fullscreen. Switch apps, run a Workflow, or search files without exiting fullscreen first.

Rename apps (free)

Rename any app on your LaunchMe grid with a custom display name. Shorten long titles, use nicknames, or label apps in your language. The rename only affects how the app appears inside LaunchMe, not the app itself in Finder.

New languages

LaunchMe now supports five additional languages:

  • Vietnamese
  • Indonesian
  • Ukrainian
  • Filipino
  • Polish

Hot Key, indexing, and cache improvements

  • Disable button for Hot Key - turn off the keyboard shortcut temporarily without resetting your shortcut
  • Redesigned app indexing - faster performance when you have 300 to 500 apps or more
  • Improved cache system - quicker reloads and smoother opens on large grids

Fixes in 1.113 through 1.118

  • Added missing Unhide app button
  • Fixed Hot Key reliability issues
  • Fixed registration UX
  • Improved overall app performance on large libraries

What's next

We are constantly improving LaunchMe and adding new features. More updates are on the way. Stay tuned for what comes next.
