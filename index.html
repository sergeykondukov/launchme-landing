As macOS Tahoe removed the original Launchpad, but we loved it so much, we decided to create our own version of it — the way it must be. It has customization options to let users make their workspace more inspiring and perfect for them. Also, you can group apps and folders for fast access to your files right from LaunchMe. Folders are also customizable, and you can set your own color, cover image, or emoji.
