Password reset is not available on this copy of the site yet. The team needs to deploy with NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_URL and NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY set, then run pnpm run build:account before publishing.

LaunchMe on the Mac App Store uses your Apple ID. There is no separate website login for the store version.

LaunchMe (Direct)

If you use LaunchMe Direct with email and password, you sign in inside the app. Password reset links from email open on this page so you can choose a new password securely.

After a successful reset, open LaunchMe (Direct) and sign in with your email and the new password.
