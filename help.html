Getting Started

Welcome to LaunchMe! This guide will help you get started with your new App Launcher for Mac.

Installation

LaunchMe is available on the Mac AppStore. Simply download and install the app to begin customizing your app launcher to bring back Launchpad experience.

First Launch

When you first open LaunchMe, you'll need to change couple settings to get "Classic View" like Launchpad was before macOS 26 Tahoe update.

First open LaunchMe and hide main Window with apps. Then go to the top tool bar "LaunchMe" and click "Settings". Navigate to the "Layout" tab and enable "Classic View"

In "Settings" you can also disable X mark "Hide button" and align Search in center of window. You also can disable page with "Categories Cards" or change settings to not show it when invoke launchme and show your apps page instead.

Key Shortcuts

By default you can invoke LaunchMe with "command 4" key shortcut. But you can change it to any keys you want.

Open "Settings" and click "Change" in "Hot key" section. Then simply press keys you want to use to invoke LaunchMe.

Customization Guide

LaunchMe offers extensive customization options to make your launcher truly yours.

Wallpapers

You can set three types of wallpapers in LaunchMe:

  • Image: Choose any image in PNG or JPG format
  • Dynamic: Wallpapers that adapt to your Dark/Light mode (HEIC format)
  • Live: Set video files in MP4, MOV, AVI, or MKV formats

Folder Customization

Create folders to organize your apps and files. Each folder can be customized with:

  • Custom colors
  • Cover images
  • Emoji icons

Icon Appearance

Choose from different icon styles: Dark, Light, or Tinted. You can also hide app names or completely hide apps you don't use frequently.

Key Features

LaunchMe comes packed with powerful features to enhance your Mac experience.

Layout Freedom

Place your apps exactly where you want them. There are no restrictions on app placement - arrange them in any pattern that works for you.

Folder Integration

Combine apps with fast access to folders. Create custom folders that give you quick access to your most important files and documents.

Liquid Glass Theme

The Liquid Glass theme gives your launcher a modern, translucent appearance. Toggle between Liquid Glass and Flat styles to match your preference.

Icon Size Control

Adjust icon sizes to better fit your screen and workflow. Find the perfect balance between visibility and screen real estate.

Troubleshooting

Having issues with LaunchMe? Here are solutions to common problems.

App Won't Launch

If LaunchMe won't open, check the following:

  • Ensure you've granted necessary permissions in System Settings
  • Check if another full-screen app is active on your display
  • Try restarting the app from the menu bar

Performance Issues

LaunchMe is optimized for minimal resource usage. If you experience performance issues:

  • Close and reopen the app
  • Check for app updates in the Mac App Store
  • Ensure your Mac meets the minimum system requirements

Wallpaper Not Displaying

If your custom wallpaper isn't showing:

  • Verify the file format is supported (PNG, JPG, HEIC, MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV)
  • Check file permissions
  • Try selecting the wallpaper again from settings

Subscriptions & Pricing

Learn about LaunchMe subscription options and how to manage your subscription.

Free Trial

You can try LaunchMe PRO for free with a 7-day trial. This gives you full access to all premium features so you can decide if LaunchMe is right for you.

PRO vs LITE

LaunchMe offers two subscription tiers:

  • PRO: Full access to all premium features including wallpapers, folder customization, hide apps, hide titles, Liquid Glass Theme, and future features
  • LITE: For users who want to support us while getting essential features like Liquid Glass, Hide Apps, and Hide Titles

Managing Your Subscription

Your subscription is managed through Apple's StoreKit. You can cancel or modify your subscription at any time through your Apple Account settings. No account registration is required - just be logged into your Apple iCloud account.

Tips & Tricks

Get the most out of LaunchMe with these helpful tips and tricks.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Customize your keyboard shortcuts in LaunchMe settings. Set a shortcut that's easy to remember and doesn't conflict with other apps.

Organizing Apps

Create logical groups of apps using folders. For example, group all your design tools together, or create a folder for your most-used applications.

Multiple Displays

LaunchMe will open on the monitor where your cursor is located. Make sure your cursor is not on a display with another full-screen app active.

Gesture Support

While LaunchMe can't use the pinch gesture directly (it's a private macOS feature), you can use third-party tools like BetterTouchTool to set up custom gestures to open LaunchMe.
